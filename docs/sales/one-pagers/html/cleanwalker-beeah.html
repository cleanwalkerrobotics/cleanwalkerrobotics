01 — The Challenge

BEEAH has set the benchmark for smart waste management in the MENA region — from the AI City Vision system on collection vehicles to the world's first fully AI-driven headquarters. BEEAH Tandeef operates city-scale cleaning across Sharjah and beyond. Yet street-level litter collection remains the last major cleaning operation untouched by automation. Across the UAE, thousands of workers perform litter picking on foot in extreme heat — covering parks, waterfronts, and public plazas manually. This gap is exactly what CleanWalker was built to close.

02 — Our Solution

CleanWalker is the world's first commercial quadrupedal litter-collecting robot, purpose-built for Gulf conditions:

  • AI vision trained on 25,000+ litter images detecting 20+ categories autonomously
  • Operates in temperatures up to 45°C with multi-shift capability (20+ hours/day)
  • Quadrupedal locomotion navigates uneven terrain, curbs, and mixed surfaces
  • Fleet management dashboard with real-time monitoring and AI City Vision integration potential

No new infrastructure required — collected waste integrates with existing collection routes.
